ABOUT PINGDOM OF MINET

Composition of Economy

The economy of the United Pingdom of Minet is broadly diversified; however, when distilled to its principal drivers, a select number of sectors emerge as the primary contributors to national prosperity.


  • Citrus Farming --> 18%

  • Sugarcane Exports --> 15%

  • Small-scaled electronics assembly --> 11%

  • Board Game Manufacturing industry --> 8%

Population Distribution

The United Pingdom of MINET has a population of 6.9 million people.This population is spread out in heterogenous manner throughout the nation.However most of the nations population is concentrated in the capital "SUSLAND"
